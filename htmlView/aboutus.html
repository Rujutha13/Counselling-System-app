General Eligibility

  • Candidates who have completed classes 8, 9, 10 11, and 12 from Tamil Nadu need not to submit any nativity proof.
  • Candidates who have completed classes 8, 9, 10 11, and 12 from states other than Tamil Nadu need to submit a nativity certificate.
  • Children or wards of Central Government Employees serving continually in Tamil Nadu for the last 5 years are required to submit parents’ certificates of employment. Proof of parents’
    employment must be submitted by candidates if their parents are employed under All India Service Tamil Nadu cadre officers.
  • Identification certificate from headquarters must be submitted by any Srilankan Tamil Refugee applicant.

Qualification Eligibility

  • Applicants must have completed class 12/HSC from any recognized boards.
  • The obligatory subjects for TNEA 2024 are Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry.
  • The candidates at least have scored 45 % (General), 40% (Backward class including backward class Muslims, MBC & DNC, and SC/SCA/ST).
  • Marine engineering applicants must have cleared class 12 with at least 60% marks in PCM and 50% in English.

Age Limit

    There is no age limit. However, marine engineering applicants must not be older than 25 years
